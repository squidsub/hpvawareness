Welcome to HPV Awareness

Stay Informed, Stay Safe

Human Papillomavirus(HPV)is a common sexually transmitted infection. More than 90 percent of sexually active men and 80 percent of sexually active women are affected by it in their lifetime.

HPV is the most common STD(sexually transmitted disease), but most of the time it isn’t a big deal. It usually goes away on its own, and most people don’t even know that they ever had HPV.

About a dozen types of HPV can sometimes lead to cancer, but two in particular (types 16 and 18) lead to the majority of cancer cases. These are called high-risk HPV.
